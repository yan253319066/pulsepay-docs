# Revenue Share Details

## What is Revenue Share?

```
Revenue Share = Share platform revenue

Activate AIP Revenue Share → Receive daily USDT/BNB dividends

Analogy:
Hold Apple stock → Get Apple company dividends
Activate AIP Revenue Share → Get PulsePay platform dividends

Difference:
- Stock dividends come from profit
- AIP dividends come from platform business revenue
```


## Revenue Calculation

### Formula

```
Personal Daily Revenue = (Personal Stake / Total Network Stake) × (Daily Platform Revenue × 40%)
```

### Example

```
Assumptions:
- Daily platform revenue: 10,000 USDT
- Revenue share ratio: 40%
- Your stake: 10,000 AIP
- Total network stake: 1,000,000 AIP

Calculation:
1. Distributable: 10,000 × 40% = 4,000 USDT
2. Your share: 10,000 / 1,000,000 = 1%
3. Your revenue: 4,000 × 1% = 40 USDT

Conclusion: Stake 10,000 AIP, get 40 USDT daily
```

### Annualized Return

```
Daily revenue: 40 USDT
Annual revenue: 40 × 365 = 14,600 USDT
Investment: 10,000 AIP (worth 10,000 USDT at time)
Annualized: 14,600 / 10,000 = 146%

⚠️ Note:
- Assumes daily revenue unchanged
- Actual revenue fluctuates
- Expected return, not guaranteed
```
