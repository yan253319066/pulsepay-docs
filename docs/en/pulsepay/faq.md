# FAQ

## About PulsePay

### What is PulsePay?

**PulsePay Protocol** is the first infrastructure protocol that converts AI usage behavior into on-chain distributable revenue. By integrating AI Gateway, crypto payment system and digital wallet, it builds a complete "AI charges → revenue accumulation → Token distribution" closed-loop economic system.

### Is PulsePay legal?

PulsePay is a technical protocol with core functions:
- Providing AI calling services
- Providing payment services
- Providing revenue sharing services

We comply with laws and regulations in operating regions and take compliance measures.

### Who is the PulsePay team?

Team information will be gradually disclosed during Phase 3. Current trust-building measures:
- Products are live and verifiable
- Contract code is open source
- Revenue is transparent on-chain
- Progressive decentralization

## About AIP Token

### What is AIP?

AIP (AI Pulse) is the governance token of PulsePay protocol. Holders can:
- Participate in revenue sharing
- Share platform business growth dividends
- Participate in protocol governance in the future

### What is the total supply of AIP?

100,000,000 AIP (100 million), fixed total supply, no inflation mechanism.

### What is the value source of AIP?

AIP has multiple value supports:
1. **Usage Value** - Stake to participate in revenue sharing
2. **Deflation Mechanism** - 30% revenue buyback and burn
3. **Demand Driven** - Users need AIP to participate in ecosystem

### What if I missed Phase 1?

After Phase 2 opens, you can purchase AIP on DEX (like PancakeSwap). At that time, the price will be determined by market supply and demand.

## About Revenue

### Where does revenue come from?

Revenue comes from PulsePay platform's real business:
- AI Gateway call fees
- Payment system processing fees

Not a Ponzi scheme, not inflation mining.

### How much is the revenue?

Revenue depends on:
- Daily platform business revenue
- Your AIP staking amount
- Total network AIP staking amount

Formula:
```
Personal Revenue = (Personal Stake / Total Network Stake) × (Daily Platform Revenue × 40%)
```

### How to claim revenue?

Click the "Claim" button manually. Revenue will be transferred to your wallet.

### Do I need to pay taxes on revenue?

Depends on your country's tax regulations. Consult a local tax advisor.

## About Staking

### What's the minimum staking amount?

Minimum 10 AIP.

### Is there a lock period?

No lock period. You can stake and unstake anytime.

### Will unstaking affect already claimed revenue?

No. Already claimed revenue belongs to you. Unstaking only affects future new revenue.

### Are there risks in staking?

Main risks:
- AIP price volatility
- Platform business decline

No:
- Liquidation risk
- Auto-deleverage risk
- Punishment mechanism
